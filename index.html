About Me

Hello, my name is Ivelisse Cintron. I am currently a student in Northwestern University's Coding Bootcamp. In six months I will be graduating from bootcamp as a full stack web developer. My motivation for completing this Bootcamp is to be in a field where I will be able to express myself creatively.

I am inspired by taking an idea in my head and making it a reality. The web development field is clearly growing at a rapid pace with many opportunities for advancement. My end goal after graduation is to become a freelance web developer. From there with using the knowledge and skills I have learned in the Bootcamp I would like to be a full time web developer.
